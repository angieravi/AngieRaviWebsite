More About Me

In my spare time, drawing is something I’ve always loved. I began sketching and doodling in middle school and naturally was no good at it. However, I enjoyed it nonetheless and found myself continuing to perfect my craft. As I got older and spent more time working on it, my skills began to get better and began to find my preferred formats and sketching utensils. Eventually, I got to a point where I began getting commissions from people close to me to make things for them and their loved ones. Overall, it’s a passion for me and I plan to continue doing it for as long as I can. It’s been fun incorporating it into the technological side of my studies.

Another thing I enjoy is cooking and baking. I’ve always liked being able to create foods and indulge in them as well. I’ve never been a picky eater, thanks to my parents making sure I try all types of foods from a young age, and I thoroughly enjoy learning how to cook all types of cuisines and following along with baking recipes. The process is very peaceful to me and I feel it’s a useful skill that you genuinely can’t really go wrong with.
